# Visual Hierarchy

The fundamental principle of UX design is Visual Hierarchy. It guides the user's eye exactly where it needs to go.

## Importance of Scale

Scale is the most effective way to establish hierarchy.

### Contrast

Contrast is the second most effective way to establish hierarchy.
