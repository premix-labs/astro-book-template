The template treats curriculum design as an engineering artifact. Start with `docs/internal/book-plan.md`, not chapter prose.

## Required Decisions

- Who is the learner and what do they already know?
- What can they build or explain after finishing?
- Which topics are deliberately excluded?
- What progressive project reveals each concept at the right time?
- Which commands and observable results prove that project works?

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid chapter lists without outcomes">
  A list of technologies is not a curriculum. Every chapter should solve a visible learner or project problem and leave a verifiable milestone.
</Callout>

## Planning Gate

Do not start drafting chapter one until `book-plan.md`, `final-project-structure.md`, relevant contracts, and the first technical decisions agree on the same outcome.
