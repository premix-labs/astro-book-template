# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors must communicate professionally, review ideas on technical merit, respect different experience levels and protect private or security-sensitive information. Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats and deliberate disruption are not accepted.

## Scope

This standard applies to issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, release coordination and other spaces where a participant represents this project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner or organization administrators. Do not publish personal data or sensitive evidence in a public issue. Security vulnerabilities follow `SECURITY.md` instead.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content, pause a discussion, reject a contribution or restrict participation. Decisions should be proportionate, documented privately when appropriate and free from retaliation against good-faith reporters.
